Why ClassRebels?

We believe that the whole idea of education is taken in the wrong sense. It’s become a misconception that studying is only about scrolling through the pages of your textbook the night before your exam. It’s not about that. Education is about freedom. Like Nelson Mandela said, “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” And that’s the dream we’re trying to fulfill. Making education not restricted to classrooms — instead of a professor talking to hundreds of students in one single auditorium, we’re providing both students and teachers with a place where they can interact with each other. Where they can study, but with a difference.
